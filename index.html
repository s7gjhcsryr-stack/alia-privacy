🔒 Alia Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

Alia Anime AI is designed as a private, on-device AI companion. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

  1. On-device AI processing

    Alia Anime AI runs a local language model on your device. The model is downloaded on first startup and requires approximately 2.4 GB of storage.

    Your conversations are processed locally on your phone and are not sent to our servers for AI processing.

  2. Information we collect

    Alia Anime AI does not require you to create an account.

    The app may store the following information locally on your device:

    • Conversation history
    • Companion memory and preferences
    • App settings
    • Downloaded AI model files

    This information is stored on your device to improve your experience and allow Alia to remember useful context.

  3. Voice input

    If voice features are enabled, the app may request microphone access.

    Microphone access is used only to capture your voice input for interaction with the app. Voice input is processed for the purpose of generating a response from Alia.

  4. Data sharing

    We do not sell your personal data.

    We do not share your conversations with advertisers or third parties.

  5. Internet access

    Internet access may be used to download the AI model during first startup or when updating the model.

    After the model is downloaded, core AI interaction is designed to run locally on your device.

  6. Data storage and deletion

    Conversation history, memory, preferences, and model files may be stored locally on your device.

    You can delete app data by removing the app from your device. Future versions may include in-app controls to clear memory and conversation history.

  7. Children's privacy

    Alia Anime AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  8. Security

    Because the app is designed to process conversations locally, your data stays on your device unless you choose to share it outside the app.

    However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Please avoid entering highly sensitive personal, financial, medical, or legal information.

  9. Changes to this policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

  10. Contact
