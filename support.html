❓ Common Questions

Does Alia run on my device?

Yes. Alia Anime AI is designed to run locally on your phone using a downloaded Gemma model.

Why does the app need storage space?

The AI model is downloaded on first startup and requires approximately 2.4 GB of device storage.

Does Alia require an internet connection?

An internet connection may be required to download the AI model during first startup or for future model updates. After the model is installed, core AI interaction is designed to run locally on your device.
